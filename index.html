Baby Keepsake (Digtial Baby Book)

This application, Baby Keepsake is goal towards new families, mothers and fathers, who want a way to document their new infants life. It serves as a reminder to take images connected to your phone calendar for appointments or the baby's first few months of life. It also serves as a reminder for unanticipated events like your baby's first steps and words. At the end of each year, you will receive a baby book in the mail that you can lookback on. In todays digital age, no one keeps baby books anymore, they have become too time consuming and overall forgetful. Currently there is no other application that is capable of this.

LNPWI - Temple Well Staff

The Temple University Lenfest North Philadelphia Workforce Initiative (templelnpwi.org) supports many different workforce actions across the North Philadelphia community. They work with many different “partners” to provide collaborative support across many diverse organizations. They also apply for multiple workforce grants to assist training, and certification programs for those who have taken an alternative path to typical education. These grants have their own reporting and tracking systems. One of these current grants is with the Pennsylvania Department of Education Grant.
